Praize Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Praize ("we," "our," or "the app") helps you identify secondhand items and estimate their resale value. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. Questions? Email downloadpraize@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Photos you scan. When you scan an item, the photo is sent to our backend for AI identification and pricing. Photos may be stored to power your scan history. You can delete them (see "Your choices").
  • Scan results. The identified item, category, condition, and estimated price range are saved to your scan history.
  • Account information. If you create an account, we store the email address and authentication details needed to sign you in. Signed-out use is supported with an anonymous identifier.
  • Barcodes. Barcodes you scan are used to look up product and pricing information.
  • Product-interaction analytics. Aggregate, non-identifying usage events (e.g. "a scan completed") to improve the app. This is not linked to your identity and is not used to track you across other apps or websites.

How your information is used

  • To identify scanned items and estimate resale value.
  • To maintain your scan history across sessions and devices.
  • To operate accounts, subscriptions, and customer support.
  • To improve the accuracy and reliability of the app.

Third parties who process your data

  • Supabase — hosts our database, storage, and backend functions.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — performs on-request AI identification of the items you scan. Images are sent for processing to return a result.
  • eBay — provides live marketplace listing and pricing data for identified items.
  • Apple — processes in-app subscription purchases; we never receive your full payment details.

We do not sell your personal information.

Camera

The app requests camera access solely to let you photograph or scan items. Camera data is used only for the scan you initiate.

Data retention

Scan history and photos are retained until you delete them or delete your account. Anonymous analytics are retained in aggregate.

Your choices

  • Clear history: Remove saved scans anytime from the History tab.
  • Delete your account: Profile → Delete Account permanently removes your account, saved scans, and stored photos from our servers.
  • Camera access: Revoke anytime in iOS Settings → Praize.

Children

Praize is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
